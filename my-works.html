Free Prompt: Zoo

Absolute madness,

A tiger is out on the loose!

While everyone is in shambles,

Why do my legs that need to run, refuse?


Instead, I pick up my pen,

Grab a stray piece of paper,

Scribble this whole poem on a whim,

About to become it's dinner.

Haiku

Harajuku town,

I wonder if you still are,

just as vibrant now.


Those good memories,

the welcoming atmosphere,

kept close in my heart.


Your colours shine bright,

regardless when day or night,

there is always light.
